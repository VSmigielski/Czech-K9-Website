OUR MISSION


We are selecting the puppies from famous Czech working bloodlines, Czech Police GSDs, Slovak Police GSDs and old Border Patrol GSDs. The best of the best.

We will be breeding 2 German Shepherd females here in our kennel in Arizona USA, and puppies will be available soon!!!

We have sons and daughters of Champion of the World IPO3 Qvido Vepeden

in our kennel. Qvido’s puppies are out of your wildest imagination about perfect German Shepherd dog. They have the speed and drive of Malinois and beauty, aggression and stronger bite of Czech German Shepherds.

One of the best protection and guard dogs in the world.

There are many breeders claiming they have Qvido Vepeden puppies. But, do they? We know the handler of Qvido Vladimír Ouška in person. We know his family, we are friends. We are not Yugoslavians or Romanians, we are all Czechs and Slovaks, we know our dogs, we have deep understanding for the German Shepherd breed. Germans coming to breed with our dogs and we go to Germany to breed with their dogs, just to preserve the best German Shepherds breed.
