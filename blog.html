What Dogs Don’t Understand

An explanation about the dangers of dogs and cars, April 16, 2019

Dogs are smart animals. They can learn many commands and dozens of tricks. They can track down humans and animals on very long distances. Hunting dogs can catch you a dinner, herding dogs can keep your sheep together and guarding dogs can protect you and keep your property from intruders. Dogs can even skateboarding. They have instincts to hide under the bushes when its raining or when its too much sunshine, they can swim, even diving to bring up objects from under the water. They are fantastic one of a kind animals. They love humans more than they love themselves, showing unconditional love……but, there are a few things they don’t understand. They just don’t understand they can get killed by running car. They also don’t understand if they jump out from running truck they can get killed. That’s why you see many dead dogs on the side of the road.

We human do understand this, so we should protect our dogs from being killed by the car.

1. When you see a stray dog running on the side of the road, or crossing the road, slow down or stop. Press your horn to scare him away, or even open your window and yell at him out, out, so he will run away. You understand that dog is not aware of being killed by the running car so you can prevent to run him over.

2. If you want to take your dog for a ride on the back of your truck, always tight him up in the middle of the truck, so he can’t jump out of the running truck and get killed. There is a penalty in California if you don’t to that, and I support this law. Dogs are good, many people are ignorant.
