Training



We train dogs of any breed and age. The options for training are in your home* or ONE ON ONE ONLINE training. Regional and International customers are welcome to join us!

We are able to provide ONE ON ONE ONLINE training. This makes receiving training for your puppy or dog extremely convenient. These sessions can be scheduled through a variety of applications that accomodate video capture such as FaceTime, Skype, WhatsApp, Signal, and others.


*If you live in Buckeye, Phoenix, Scottsdale, Peoria, Tempe, Chandler, or between the cities mentioned, we will come to your home to train your dog!

Background and History of the Trainer


Hi, my name is Dee Dee. I am a dog trainer who has over 30 years of experience training dogs. I have titled several dogs with the Schutzhund title. I have mastered dog psychology in early years. I understand the work that goes into training a dog. I strongly believe that any dog is trainable if you know how. And I know how.
